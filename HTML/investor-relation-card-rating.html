    Credit Ratings

    Our ratings and associated research help investors analyze the credit risks associated with fixed-income securities by providing detailed information of the ability of issuers to meet their obligations.

    Zenith Bank Investor Relations Credit Rating

    Credit Ratings

    A credit rating is an assessment of the solvency or credit-worthiness of debtors and/or bond-issuers according to established credit review procedures.

    For the issuers of fixed income securities, credit ratings can provide greater market liquidity for their securities and reduced transaction costs. A rating is continuously monitored as it enables investors to measure their investment risk.
