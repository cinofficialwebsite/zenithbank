    The Zenith Individual Savings Account is recommended if you have a specific savings goal in mind or you just want to start setting money aside. This account offers interest on your funds regardless of the goal.

    • Zero account opening balance

    • MasterCard/Visa/Verve debit card
    • Internet banking
    • Zenith mobile banking app
    • Email and/or SMS alertz

    REQUIREMENTS

    • Account opening form duly completed
    • One recent clear passport photograph of signatory
