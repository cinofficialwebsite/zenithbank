    Zenith International Money Transfer

    Receive foreign currency transfer from abroad using international money transfers

    Zenith Bank IMT

    The Zenith International Money Transfer Direct to Account Service

    You can receive foreign currency transfers from abroad using your Zenith Bank Domiciliary & Naira account details. Visit any of our dedicated remittance branches across the nation for your remittance transactions and enquiries.

    Benefits

    • The pace at which the money is transferred within the comfort of one’s home or office.
    • The money can be transferred directly to a bank account.
    • Customer service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
    • Online account will save all transaction history.
    • There are no hidden fees charges.
    • Customers can receive up to $10,000 in cash. For Zenith Customers without a Dollar account - A USD account will be automatically created to receive funds directly to the account.

    Features

    • Low Cost
    • Secure
    • Convenient
    • Reliability
    • Flexibility
    • Speed

    Requirements

    • Transaction PIN
    • Expected Amount in USD
    • Acceptable means of identification
    • Sender’s name and address
    • Receiver’s name, address, and phone number

    Pick-up Cash in US Dollars
    Non-account holders can also pick-up cash in US Dollars at any of our branches.

    Acceptable Means of Identification

    • BVN
    • Nigerian International Passport/Foreign Passport
    • Driver’s License
    • Permanent Voters card
    • NIMC card


    Eligibility
    This service is available to both account holders and walk-in-customers who are above 18 years.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What is International Money Transfers?
    International money Transfers is a platform designed for Nigerians who do not reside in the country to have access to a wide range of Money transfers Operators

    2. Who is eligible for this service?
    Non-Account holder and Account holders.

    3. What electronic channels and services are available for transfers Customers?
    The following channels are available to our customers:

    a) Cash Pick Up: Customers can walk into any of our locations and pick up cash across the counter.

    b) Direct to Account: Funds can be sent directly into your Domiciliary account.

    4. Who do I contact for complaints?
    You can contact our Money Transfer Department via mail on info@customercarezenith.com. Alternatively, you can call 234-9039790631, 234--9168856956

    5. Why did the operator say my reference number is invalid?
    Contact your Sender to investigate with International Money Transfer Operators.

    6. Why is the rate of conversion for transactions different from the CBN/parallel market rate?
    Zenith Bank does not determine the rate of conversion for the transactions. The rates are automated on the software and are not customized for manipulation by the Bank.

    7. Can I collect my transfers on weekends?
    Zenith Bank has over 350 dedicated centers spread across the country that offer Money Transfer services every day except weekends and public holidays from the hours of 8am – 4pm.

    8. What do I do if my transfer has been locked to a Zenith Bank location?
    Contact your Sender to effect its release with the Agent partner from the sending country.

    9. Why did the operator keep a copy of my form after refusing payment?
    The operator keeps the original copy of the form as a proof that the customer visited the location and the transfer was not paid based on the reason(s) annotated on the form.

    10. Can I receive funds as a company or to a company?
    You cannot receive funds as a company. The CBN policy states that the inbound service is strictly on “a person to person basis”. No business transactions are allowed.

    11. I used my Naira account details to receive funds.
    Go to any of our branches and request for customer service to use your naira account to check for your accounts.

    Contact

    Email: info@customercarezenith.com

    Money Transfer Operators

    Zenith International money Transfer platform is designed to give you access to a wide range of Money transfer Operators.

    Western Union

    Western Union money transfer is an electronic funds transfer service that enables people send & receive money from over 250 countries and Western Union Agent locations around the World and receive them in over 370 Zenith Bank branches nation-wide.

    Requirements for Cash Pick up

    • The Money Transfer Control Number (MTCN)
    • Expected Amount
    • Acceptable means of identification
    • The receiver’s name, address and phone numbers
    • Sender’s name and country (city to be included for transfers coming from the US and Mexico)
    • Test Question and Answer where applicable.

    MONEYGRAM

    With Zenith’s MoneyGram services, you are assured of receiving your money from Monday through Friday nationwide and from selected locations. The product is uniquely designed to allow both customers and non-customers of Zenith Bank receive funds.

    Beneficiaries can receive MoneyGram at any of our branches nationwide by providing the following:

    • Transfer Reference Number.
    • Expected Amount.
    • Verified BVN.
    • Sender’s name and country.
    • Test Question and Answer (optional).

    Fully completed form.

    WORLDREMIT

    Zenith Bank Plc is in partnership with World Remit (a global money transfer and payment company) with network spanning over 58 countries and 125 destinations. The company offers an online money transfer service that provides international remittance services to expatriates and migrant workers. Your loved ones can send you money online and you can receive directly to your bank account.

    Worldremit offers a value added payment service (Business to Business B2B, Business to Person B2P and Person to Person P2P) to beneficiaries in Nigeria.

    Lead Remit (First Apple)

    Leadremit allows individuals living in the US to send funds to their family, friends and associates in Nigeria. Funds can be received in US dollars’ cash in over 370 Zenith Bank branches nation-wide.

    Global Currency is the faster, safer way to receive money from your loved ones, family and friends.

    SENDWAVE

    SMALLWORLD
